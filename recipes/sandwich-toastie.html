Indian Sandwich Toastie

Description

A classic british sandwhich toastie, toasted in a sandwhich toaster

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 100g shreddar cheddar
  • 1/2 chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 chopped oninion
  • 1/2 chopped bell pepper
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 tbsp garam masala
  • 1/4 tbsp kishmiri chilli powder

Steps

  1. Slather the bread with butter
  2. Add the shredded cheese to one slice of bread
  3. Layer the tomatoes, onionion, bell pepper on top of cheese
  4. Add a sprinkle of salt of top
  5. Add spices on top
  6. Place top slice on top
  7. Put the sandwich in the toaster for 5-8 mins until golden
  8. Slice and enjoy
